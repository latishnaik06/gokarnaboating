Sightseeing

Explore the stunning beaches and attractions of Gokarna. Each destination promises an unforgettable experience filled with beauty and tranquility.

Om Beach

Experience the iconic Om-shaped shoreline, perfect for a relaxing and peaceful getaway.

Kudle Beach

Unwind on this crescent-shaped beach, known for its golden sands and calm waters.

Half Moon Beach

A secluded paradise offering a perfect blend of privacy and natural beauty.

Paradise Beach

A hidden gem accessible only by trek or boat, perfect for adventurers.

Tadadi Harbour

A bustling fishing port where you can witness local life and scenic surroundings.

Light House

Climb to the top for panoramic views of Gokarna and the surrounding coastline.

Mystery Cave

Discover the hidden wonders of this fascinating cave near the coastline.

Dolphin Watching

Set out on a boat ride to witness dolphins playing in their natural habitat.
